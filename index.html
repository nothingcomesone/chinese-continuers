个人 · 自己与家庭 ★ 考试重点Personal Identity

1
请你先介绍一下你自己。
qǐng nǐ xiān jiè shào yī xià nǐ zì jǐ。
Please introduce yourself first.
范文 · model answer
我是一个比较安静但做事认真的学生。虽然平时不太爱说话，但是我特别喜欢和朋友一起做小组项目，因为大家一起讨论、互相帮助，不但能学得更好，而且让我感觉更自信了。
wǒ shì yí gè bǐ jiào ān jìng dàn zuò shì rèn zhēn de xué shēng。suī rán píng shí bù tài ài shuō huà，dàn shì wǒ tè bié xǐ huān hé péng yǒu yì qǐ zuò xiǎo zǔ xiàng mù，yīn wèi dà jiā yì qǐ tǎo lùn、hù xiāng bāng zhù，bù dàn néng xué dé gèng hǎo，ér qiě ràng wǒ gǎn jué gèng zì xìn le。
I’m a quiet but hardworking student. Although I don’t speak much in class, I really enjoy doing group projects with friends because we discuss ideas and help each other — not only does this help me learn better, but it also makes me feel more confident.
认真 rèn zhēn · serious/hardworking小组项目 xiǎo zǔ xiàng mù · group project自信 zì xìn · confidence
